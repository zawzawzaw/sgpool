1968
Founded

Introduced TOTO which was sold at standalone booths.

1969
Singapore Sweep

Introduced Singapore Sweep with $400,000 as the first prize.

1972
Colombo Court Office

Moved from first office in Percival Road to Columbo Court.

1973
National Stadium

Contributed S$14.5 million to its construction cost.

1980
Temasek Holdings

Became subsidiary of Temasek Holdings.

1986
4D + Computerisation Drive

Launched 4-Digit number game and moved from manual operations to computerised gaming system.

1988
Singapore Indoor Stadium

Sponsored S$45 million to its construction cost.

International Plaza

Moved office to International Plaza.

1991
National Day Parade

Supported the National Day Parade since.

1992
4D Sweep

Inaugurated 4D-Sweep, a 4D game based on the Singapore Sweep draw results.

1993
NCSS Platinum Award

Awarded with NCSS Platinum Award for contributions to the Sports Excellence 2000 (SPEX 2000) programme.

SPEX 2000

Supported the development and nurturing of sports talents in Singapore.

1999
Football Betting

Launched football betting on the S. League in support of professional football development in Singapore.

2000
Selegie Road

Moved office from Paradiz Centre at Selegie Road.

TOTO Hongbao Draw

Conducted the first TOTO Hongbao Draw, reaching a jackpot prize of $11 million.

2002
International Football Betting

Introduced football betting for international matches.

Esplanade

Funded the construction of Esplanade - Theatres on the Bay.

2003
iShine

Launched iShine, the staff community programme.

2004
Tote Board

Became a subsidiary of Tote Board, took over Singapore Turf Club branches and started conducting 4D Draws.

2005
Phone Betting

Launched telephone betting.

2007
Responsible
Gaming
Programme

Launched the programme to create a responsible gaming environment for all.

2008
Formula One Betting

Offered sports betting on Formula One races.

2010
Livewire

Set up LiveWire betting venues.

2011
Gardens By The Bay

Sponsored the opening of Gardens by the Bay.

2012
Level 4 Accreditation

Achieved Level 4 of World Lottery Association Responsible Gaming Workframe, the highest global standard of responsible gaming.

2013
Singapore Pools Building

Officially opened the 12-storey Singapore Pools Building at Middle Road on 28 April and it won BCA Green Mark GoldPlus Award for Singapore Pools Building.

2015
National Art Gallery

Sponsored the restoration and opening of the National Art Gallery.

2016
World Lottery Summit

Co-hosted the World Lottery Summit from 6 to 9 Nov, the second time in 10 years.
