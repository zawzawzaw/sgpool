Singapore Pools is 50!

This year, Singapore Pools celebrates 50 years for community benefit.

Our 50th celebration will bring together our stakeholders spanning the public, customers, corporate partners and staff to join us on our Golden Jubilee journey.

Singapore Pools has been around to provide safe and trusting betting to counter illegal gambling, and to help build an inclusive and caring community.
